Payment Methods in China

Important: China is Nearly Cashless!

Most places in China no longer accept cash. You MUST have mobile payment methods set up.

WeChat Pay

Most popular payment method

Setup Steps:

  1. Download WeChat app
  2. Register with foreign phone number
  3. Add international credit card
  4. Verify identity with passport
  5. Link bank account or use Tour Pass
Tip: Use WeChat Tour Pass for short-term visitors

Alipay

Alternative to WeChat Pay

Setup Steps:

  1. Download Alipay app
  2. Register with foreign phone number
  3. Add international credit card
  4. Use Alipay Tour Card for visitors
Tip: Alipay is more foreigner-friendly than WeChat Pay

International Cards

Limited acceptance

Where Accepted:

  • High-end hotels
  • International stores (Starbucks, McDonald's)
  • Some tourist attractions
  • Airports and train stations
Warning: Most local restaurants and shops don't accept foreign cards

üö® Emergency Payment Options

  • Bank Transfers: Go to Bank of China with passport for cash advance
  • Western Union: Available in major cities
  • Hotel Concierge: Can help with payment issues
  • Tourist Hotline: 12301 for assistance
